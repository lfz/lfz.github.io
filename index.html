About Me

Since entering Tsinghua University in 2009 (Chemical Biology, B.S.; Biomedical Engineering, Ph.D.), I have dedicated my career to the intersection of life sciences and information science. My doctoral research focused on pioneering AI algorithms for medical imaging, tackling challenges in 3D convolutional neural networks, weakly supervised learning, adversarial robustness, and multimodal fusion.

Currently, I am the CEO of fastdiag.ai. Previously, as the Chief AI Scientist at Shukun Technology, I built a 60+ person cross-disciplinary AI team from the ground up and established a complete pipeline from data governance to commercial deployment. My work aims to translate cutting-edge AI research into clinically impactful and commercially successful medical devices.
